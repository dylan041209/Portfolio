Project 01 / Business coursework

Marketing campaign for Top Tier Pizza Co.

A marketing exam project that turned research, strategy and planning into a focused campaign for a fictional pizza business.

The brief

Designing a campaign with a clear route to results.

For my marketing exam, I created a complete campaign for Top Tier Pizza Co. The work focused on understanding the market first, then using that insight to make practical, informed decisions.

Project typeMarketing exam
FocusStrategy & planning
BusinessTop Tier Pizza Co.

What I created

01

Competitor research

Explored what works in the pizza industry, using competitor research to identify useful approaches and opportunities.

02

SMART aims & objectives

Set specific, measurable, achievable, relevant and time-bound goals to give the campaign a clear direction.

03

Business analysis

Considered what Top Tier Pizza Co. does well, so the campaign could build on its strengths.

04

Budget & timeline

Allocated a budget and created a timeline to make the best possible use of the campaign resources and maximise results.

Key takeaway

Good marketing starts with insight, then makes every decision count.
